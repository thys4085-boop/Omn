About OMN

Driving sustainable energy solutions and infrastructure development across South Africa

Leading Energy & Infrastructure Solutions

OMN Projects (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic South African company registered in 2015 (Reg. No. 2015/200631/07), specializing in energy projects, petroleum solutions, and infrastructure development. Based in Randburg, Johannesburg, we are committed to delivering excellence in every project we undertake.
